# Privacy Policy for Tunefy

**Effective Date: May 2, 2026**

Tunefy ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application, Tunefy (the "App").

### 1. Information We Collect

*   **Local Data:** Tunefy is designed to be a local-first application. We do not collect or store any of your personal data on our servers. All information, including your playlists, liked songs, and playback history, is stored locally on your device using browser-based storage (LocalStorage).
*   **Audio Files:** The App allows you to scan and play audio files stored on your device. We do not upload or share these files.
*   **Device Information:** We may collect non-personal information about your device, such as device model and OS version, to improve app performance and compatibility.

### 2. How We Use Your Information

*   To provide and maintain the App's core functionality (playback, playlists, history).
*   To personalize your experience.
*   To show advertisements (via Google AdMob).

### 3. Third-Party Services

*   **Google AdMob:** We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data (like your device's advertising ID) to show relevant ads. Please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for more details.

### 4. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:
*   **Storage/Files:** To scan and play your local music files.
*   **Media Session:** To provide lock screen and background playback controls.

### 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
